I have 3 cute male cats that I have been taking care of since they were born. I named them Juma, Puma and Acil.

Juma is the eldest son who has the full name "Jumanji" (because he is an active child). Puma has the full name "Purnama" (because he was born during the full moon phenomenon and has a black patch over his right eye). Juma and Puma are 6 months apart. And the youngest is Acil. Acil has the full name "Achilles" (at birth he has the largest body compared to his siblings). Puma and Acil differ by 1 year.

They have a different nature. Juma is the most aggressive. Little Juma was very agile and he wasn't afraid to even scratch/attack me. Puma has a very affectionate nature. Puma often licks his younger sibling and accompanies him to sleep. Meanwhile, Acil is the most spoiled, maybe because he's still a baby.
